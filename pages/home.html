JozzDNDSheet

Started somewhere around August 2025 as a personal project and built session by session into something actually worth using. This is version 1.0 — the point where it stopped being a work in progress and started being a real sheet. It will keep changing, things will get added, stuff will break and get fixed, but this is the line in the sand. Everything from here gets tracked properly.
