Ability Scores — the six core stats that drive everything.

Enter the raw score (e.g. 16) and the Bonus field calculates automatically: (score − 10) ÷ 2, rounded down. A 16 STR gives +3, a 9 DEX gives −1.

Combat Stats below auto-fill from your scores:

— Initiative = DEX bonus. Type to override (e.g. Alert feat adds +5).

— Proficiency Bonus = your level (levels 1–4: +2, 5–8: +3, 9–12: +4, 13–16: +5, 17–20: +6). Read-only.

— Passive Perception = 10 + Perception bonus. Type to override, use ↺ to reset.

— AC and Speed are manual — enter from your equipment and race.

Saving Throws — auto-calculated from ability bonus + proficiency if the checkbox is ticked. Tick it if your class grants proficiency in that save.

Skills — your character's trained abilities, each tied to one of the six ability scores.

Checkbox — tick if your class or background grants proficiency in this skill. Adds your Proficiency Bonus to the total.

Total (left box, read-only) — auto-calculated: ability modifier + proficiency bonus (if proficient). Updates automatically whenever your scores or level change.

Adj (right box) — your manual adjustment. Use this for item bonuses, spell effects, Bard's Jack of All Trades, Expertise, or any situational modifier. Positive or negative, e.g. +2 or −1.

The total shown is what you roll for skill checks unless you have a special modifier from a feature.

Health — track your HP, death saves, rests, hit dice, and potions.

Max HP / Current HP — enter max from your class and CON modifier. Current HP updates live. Use +1/−1 buttons or Custom for damage and healing.

Temp HP — managed separately via the Custom button. Damage hits temp HP first; it doesn't stack with other temp HP sources.

Death Saves — click the circles to track successes (3 = stabilise) and failures (3 = dead). Reset automatically on Long Rest.

Short Rest — spend hit dice to recover HP. Enter how many to spend and click Short Rest. Resets short-rest spell slots and resources.

Long Rest — fully restores HP and death saves. Resets all spell slots and resources that recover on long rest.

Hit Dice Recovery — choose your hit die size (from your class) and how many to spend. CON modifier is auto-synced from your CON score above.

Health Potions — select a potion type, click Use Potion three times to confirm. Rolls the dice and applies healing automatically.

Proficiencies & Training — a free-text notepad for everything your character is trained in.

Use it to list: weapon and armour proficiencies, tool proficiencies (e.g. Thieves' Tools, Herbalism Kit), languages, and any special training from your background or class.

This is a reference field only — it doesn't affect any calculations. Keep it as brief or detailed as you like.

Actions, Bonus Actions & Features — everything your character can do, split into three lists by when it happens.

Actions = things on your turn (attacks, most spells). Bonus Actions = bonus-action stuff (off-hand attack, some spells/features). Features & Traits = passive or always-on abilities (racial traits, class features).

Add from sheet — searches your own weapons, spells, items and racial traits and drops them in ready-made. Flip the "Search everything in D&D 5e" toggle to pull from the whole ruleset instead. Spells automatically land in the right section based on their casting time.

Add custom — write your own card by hand: name, timing (which section), damage, range, uses, and an optional reference link that shows a "View rules" button.

Cards — long descriptions collapse with a "View more" toggle; a coloured badge shows where a pulled card came from (weapon / spell / item / trait). Star ❤️ to pin favourites to the top; ✏️ edit or 🗑️ delete any card.

Nothing here changes your inventory, weapons or other characters — pulling just copies a ready-to-edit card into your list.

Weapons — your weapons, their attack details, and a fair dice roller.

Add from sheet pulls any D&D 5e weapon with its dice + type pre-filled; Add custom is pure homebrew. Either way you can Edit afterwards and change anything.

Roll buttons on each card: Attack — 🎯 normal, ADV (advantage), DIS (disadvantage); Damage — 🎲 normal, CRIT. Every single die is shown in the Roll Log below, natural 20s and 1s are highlighted, and rolls use true random so they're perfectly fair. The Roll Log is saved to your sheet.

To-hit auto-calculates from your chosen ability (STR/DEX) + proficiency (toggle it off for a non-proficient weapon). Magic bonus (+1 to +5) adds to both attack and damage and shows on the name (e.g. "Longsword +2"). For homebrew, use the to-hit / damage overrides or a custom dice expression to force exact numbers.

Long Term Conditions — persistent status effects on your character.

Use this for conditions from spells, diseases, curses, or ongoing effects: Poisoned, Blinded, Charmed, Restrained, etc.

Each condition card shows its name, how many turns it lasts (or Indefinite), and a description of the effect. Click Remove when it ends.

Conditions are saved with your character and persist across sessions.

Quick Notes — a scratchpad for anything that doesn't have its own field.

Good uses: reminders about class features, things to ask your DM, current quest objectives, spell concentration reminders, NPC names, or anything you need to jot mid-session.

This is a free-text area — no formatting, just type. Saved with your character automatically.
