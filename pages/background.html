Backstory — your character's history, origin, and story before the campaign began.

Write as much or as little as you like. Common things to include: where your character grew up, what drove them to adventure, key events from their past, important relationships, and how they got their class abilities.

This is a free-text field — no formatting rules. Saves automatically.

Background Fields — customisable text boxes for any aspect of your character's background and personality.

Default boxes include: Personality Traits, Ideals, Bonds, Flaws, Appearance, and more. Each is a free-text area — write as much as you like.

+ Add Box — create a new custom field with any title you choose (e.g. "Gods & Religion", "Party Relationships", "Known Languages", "Secrets").

Reorder Mode — drag the boxes into any order you prefer. Click Reorder Mode again to exit.

Each box has a delete button in reorder mode. Default boxes (Traits, Ideals, Bonds, Flaws) cannot be deleted — they are part of the standard D&D 5e character sheet.

Everything saves automatically as you type.
