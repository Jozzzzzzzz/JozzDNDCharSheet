Equipment — armour, shields, cloaks, and worn gear that affects your defence or combat stats.

This list is shared with the Stats page — anything added here also appears in the Equipment box on the Stats tab. You only need to enter it once.

Each item has a name, type, AC bonus/value, weight (lbs), and optional notes. Use the Notes button to record attunement status, magical properties, or flavour text.

Equipment weight is totalled at the bottom and counts toward your encumbrance limit if encumbrance is enabled in Main Inventory.

Main Inventory — everything your character is carrying that isn't worn equipment.

Max Weight Capacity — optional. Set this to your carrying capacity (usually STR score × 15 lbs in 5e). Leave at 0 to disable the limit check.

D&D 5e Encumbrance Rules — tick to enable tier-based encumbrance: Encumbered (STR × 5 lbs, −10 ft speed), Heavily Encumbered (STR × 10 lbs, −20 ft speed, disadvantage on ability checks), Max (STR × 15 lbs, can't move). The status label updates live.

Currency — track Copper, Silver, and Gold here. Add custom currencies (Electrum is shown separately, or add your own homebrew coin). Currency is saved per character.

+ Add Item — opens the item form. Fill in name, type, weight, quantity, and value. Tick Stackable if it's a consumable you buy multiples of (arrows, rations, potions) — stackable items get a + button to record purchases and deduct currency automatically.

Filter bar — type to instantly filter items by name.

Total carried weight is shown at the bottom, updating as items are added or removed.

Additional Storage — create named containers to organise items separately from your main inventory. Good for: a Bag of Holding, a cart, a campsite chest, a familiar's pack, a shared party stash.

Click + Add Additional Storage to create a container. Give it a name and an optional max weight limit (0 = unlimited).

Each container gets its own item list, filter bar, and weight total — same as Main Inventory but separate. Items can be moved between containers using the Move dropdown on each item card.

Delete a container with the Delete Container button — you'll be asked to confirm. Items inside are removed too, so move them first if you want to keep them.

Purchase History — a log of every time you used the + refill button on a stackable item and chose to record a purchase.

Each entry shows: date, item name, quantity bought, cost per unit, total cost, and which currency was spent. Useful for tracking how much gold you've spent across a session or campaign.

Currency is automatically deducted from your wallet when you confirm a purchase through the refill popup.

Clear History — removes the log entries only. Your items and currency are not affected.
